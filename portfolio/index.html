Game Modification: ARMED
Armed is a card game where each player duels to deplete their opponent's deck! Each player is dealt a 26-card deck and are then tasked with playing cards with values higher than their opponent’s. The victor of each battle takes both cards. Once one player can no longer draw from their deck, the other player with more cards reigns victorious!

(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: Pixelfight TCG
Pixel FIGHT! PixelFIGHT is a 2-player PvP sandbox trading card game inspired by similar games like Yu-Gi-Oh, Magic the Gathering, Minecraft, and Terraria. In PixelFIGHT, players can craft the strongest structures and the hardiest tools. The player who destroys all their opponent’s Structures and depletes their HP to 0 reigns victorious!

In Trick or Treat Simulator, you are Ghost Kid. On Halloween Night 1985, you traverse trick or treating, friendship, defeating schoolyard bullies, and love.
